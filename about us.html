About us

 Offering guests the best in quality and service has been the primary principle at GADGET ZONE since its inception
by Mr. Nanu Gupta, a visionary with astute business acumen and foresight. A marvellous journey that began with a small Electronics Showroom at Mahim in 1967,
GADGET ZONE has since evolved and has become one of India’s biggest retail chains.
GADGET ZONE caters to guests of all budgets and preferences.
Today, you can shop for the best in Electronics from 117 aesthetically-designed and conveniently located showrooms
in cities like Mumbai, New Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Surat, Vadodara, Ahmedabad, Delhi, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Indirapuram,
Noida, Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Warangal, Vishakhapatnam, Tirupati, Kakinada & Rajahmundry .
Along with a dedicated team of prolific managers, Mr. Nanu Gupta and his sons Nilesh Gupta and Ashish Gupta stand
tall on the promise of serving their guests with the absolute best in both product and after-sales service.
Bade Sir’s guest-focussed initiatives and intuitive understanding of emerging markets have helped
GADGET ZONE to become a household name and the first preference for many when it comes to quality electronics
and consumer durables.
